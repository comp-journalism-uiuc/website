News Aggregator

A Twitter Bot that scrapes articles for trending Google searches, clusters them, selects the most central article in each cluster, and tweets out the title and link to the article.

About 1000 articles are scraped and about 15 are selected per day. The data is available below:
All Articles
Selected Articles
Note on the data: the topic column is the Google Search the scraped article was associated with.
